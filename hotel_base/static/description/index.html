HEMFA Hotel Base

A secure, multi-property foundation for consistent hotel operations in Odoo 19.

Hotel Base centralizes the people, properties, room inventory and access rules that every HEMFA PMS workflow depends on. Configure the hotel once, keep operational data consistent, and give each team access only to the information needed for its work.

Everything starts with reliable hotel master data

Properties and business days

Manage multiple hotel properties under one Odoo company. Each property has its own code, address, timezone, operational date and configurable business-day start for accurate daily hotel processing.

Rooms and inventory structure

Organize floors, room types, individual room numbers and amenities. Track capacity, housekeeping condition, physical occupancy, out-of-order blocks and house-use rooms from one dependable inventory model.

Guest identity profiles

Extend the standard Odoo contact with hotel-ready guest details, including nationality, date and place of birth, profession, national number and supported identity-document types.

Agencies and billing entities

Distinguish travel agencies, companies and government entities from individual guests. Associate guests with their usual entity and prepare clean master data for routed billing, advances and future commission workflows.

Property-scoped security

Assign one or more allowed properties and a default property to every hotel user. Record rules and role-specific fields help prevent cross-property access and keep sensitive guest identity data away from unauthorized teams.

Workflow-controlled room states

Reservation, housekeeping and maintenance workflows update room status through controlled operations. This protects sellable inventory from accidental manual changes and gives downstream dashboards a consistent source of truth.

Designed around real hotel controls

  • Company and property consistency checks across core records
  • Unique property room numbers and property-specific room types
  • Sellable-room calculation that excludes maintenance and house use
  • Timezone-aware operational dates and configurable day rollover
  • Tracked changes for important property and room information
  • Separate roles for front desk, cashier, supervisors, housekeeping, maintenance, F&B, accounting and management

Foundation for the complete HEMFA PMS

Hotel Base supplies the shared property, room, partner and security structure used by the rest of the suite. Add reservation management, the live room board, folios, cashier sessions, rate management, housekeeping, maintenance, guest services, night audit, POS room charges and bilingual reporting as operational needs grow.

Built for Odoo 19 Enterprise and designed for a single-company, multi-property hotel environment with Odoo Accounting as the financial source of truth.
